Policy Statment

At Terra Magna Holdings LLC, we specialize in the acquisition of vacant land for various purposes. However, it is important to note that there is no obligation for Terra Magna Holdings LLC to purchase any particular piece of land, nor is there any obligation for a client to sell land to us.

If a client wishes to engage in a transaction with Terra Magna Holdings LLC, they are free to do so at their own discretion. If a client chooses to use their own legal or professional contacts to conduct any part of the deal, Terra Magna Holdings LLC will not be responsible for covering any costs associated with those services. We encourage clients to use their own legal and professional contacts if they feel more comfortable doing so.

Our goal at Terra Magna Holdings LLC is to facilitate fair and mutually beneficial transactions for both parties involved. We value the autonomy and agency of our clients and respect their right to make decisions about their land and financial dealings.
