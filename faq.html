Frequently Asked Questions

Does this cost me any money?

No. We cover all costs associated with the deal. The offer amount you receive from us is the amount you will take home.

Is your offer fair?

Yes. Our offer takes into account us covering all costs associated with the deal. We charge no commissions, cover all administrative and closing costs, and can close fast because we are offering an all cash deal. Using an agent to sell your land could take months, and leave you responsible for costs such as property taxes, commissions and closing costs.

How do you determine the price?

We look at comparable properties in the area. We look at how much they are selling for, how fast they are selling, and what they may sell for in the future. We factor in all of the costs we will incur in closing the deal and marketing to a new buyer to get a baseline price. Once we have a baseline price we will factor in our profit for the service we are offering.

What's the catch?

There is none! We offer a no obligation service to you. Our goal as a direct buyer is to buy unused land and resell it to someone who can use it to its full potential. We aim to provide a service that is mutually beneficial. You are in complete control in choosing whether or not to sell your property.
